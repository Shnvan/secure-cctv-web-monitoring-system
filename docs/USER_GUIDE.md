# User Guide

## Viewer

1. Connect to the approved private network or access gateway.
2. Authenticate with your approved account and MFA/passkey.
3. Open the private app address.
4. Select an assigned camera.
5. If the camera is offline, notify the admin.
6. Log out or disconnect when finished.

Rules:

- Do not share screenshots or feeds unless approved.
- Do not share access links or tokens.
- Report unexpected access or strange behavior.
- Use only approved devices.

## Phone camera source

1. Connect phone to the approved private network.
2. Open the camera publisher page.
3. Authenticate as the assigned camera-source identity.
4. Permit camera access when browser prompts.
5. Keep phone plugged in.
6. Do not use the phone for other browsing during demo.
